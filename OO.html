Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c oats (can be old fashioned, steel cut, or a mix of both)
  • 1 scoop of vanilla protein powder
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp ground flax seed
  • 1 tbsp of nuts (I like using pumpkin seeds or walnuts)
  • 1/2 c milk of choice
  • 1 tbsp nut butter
  • Fruit of choice (I love blueberries and raspberries)

Steps

  1. Add dry ingredients to a mason jar and stir to combine
  2. Add in milk and peanut butter and stir until well combined
  3. You can add in fruit before sealing or use them as topping afterwards or both!
  4. Store in fridge overnight or until it becomes more solid consistency

Other Options

  • Add grated carrots, raisins, and walnuts to make carrot cake overnight oats!
  • Add grated zucchini and walnuts to make zucchini bread overnight oats!
