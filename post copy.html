Date Balls

General Ingredients

  • 1 c nuts such as:
    • Almonds
    • Pecans
    • Walnuts
  • 1 1/2 c dried fruit such as:
    • Dates
    • Apples
    • Cherries
    • Raisins

Steps

  1. Blend nuts in food processor until they become gravel texture
  2. Add rest of ingredients into food processor until desired consistency
  3. Form into shape of balls or bars

Specific recipe used in this photo:

  • 1/2 c pitted medjool dates
  • 1/2 c goji berries
  • 3/4 c walnuts
  • 1/4 c sunflower seeds
  • 1/4 c pepitas
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Cinnamon to taste

Notes

  • Can be refrigerated or left at room temperature in airtight container

Photograph by Sydney Pexton

The Goodness:

This snack is the key to balancing glucose levels in your body! Why? Because the fiber found in the dried fruits creates a lining in your gut that slows the absorption of glucose into the bloodstream. The protein from the nuts slows the process of glucose getting to your gut.

Why does this matter? Most people experience drastic drops or spikes in their glucose levels daily without even realizing it. This leads to inflammation which impacts mental health and increases the risk of several diseases. It can lead to insulin resistance which results in type 2 diabetes. It can also cause issues with hormones. All of these things are a recipe for not feeling so great. And no one wants that! But with this simple healthy snack, your body will be able to better manage those glucose levels and keep you feeling great!
